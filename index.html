Currently I am in my second year of completing my Bachelors degree in Computer Science. Majoring in computer science here at RIT has proven to be tough but engaging. I have had some harsh professors and some great professors. With my Computer Science degree I plan on being a Front-End Developer or a Software Engineer.

These are the courses I am currently taking:

  • Introduction to Software Engineering: A course that is meant to introduce students to general topics in software engineering, and have students practice that knowledge by working on a term-long team-based project.

  • Biology II: This course serves as an introduction to animal and plant anatomy and physiology, in addition to the fundamentals of ecology.
