Beachtown Activities

Peru, located in South America, boasts a rich cultural history with notable sites like Machu Picchu and the Nazca Lines. And notably Peru's architecture, art, food, and music reflect diverse global influences.

The Lima Main Square features notable landmarks like the Government Palace, the Lima Metropolitan Cathedral, and the Archbishop's Palace, offering visitors the chance to explore historical architecture and religious art.

The Mercado Central in Lima is a popular, expansive market situated in the heart of the city, occupying a whole city block. Inside, visitors can explore a vibrant food market with fresh produce and a variety of meats, seafood, and local dishes prepared by vendors.

Miraflores in Lima is a renowned tourist destination boasting stunning coastal views, upscale shopping, and diverse culinary delights. Its iconic clifftop location offers panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean with multiple attractions.
